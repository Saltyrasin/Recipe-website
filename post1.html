Dried Mango and Cranberry Parfait:
restaurant makes - 1 Serving
timer Total Time - 5 min.

Recipe: Yogurt Parfait



Parfaits make an easy snack for a sunny afternoon or if you want a quick breakfast. Mango and cranberry is the perfect combo and makes for a crunchy and chewy parfait!


Ingredients:


  • ½ Cup - Yogurt
  • 1 Slice - Dried Mango
  • 1 tablespoon - Cranberries
  • ¼ Cup - Granola

First dice up both the dried mango and cranberries (about ¼ in cubes). Then in a ramekin or glass add and yogurt and your granola. Mix until evenly distributed without breaking up the chunks of granola. Lastly add both the Mango and Cranberry cubes on top and top with a drizzle of honey for sweetness.
